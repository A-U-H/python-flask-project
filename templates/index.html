Frequently AsQked Questions

Everything you need to know about scaling your brand with Ecom Squire. We provide the clarity you need to move forward with confidence.

How quickly can I expect to see results in my sales and ACOS?

Initial optimizations to listings and SEO typically show results within 14 to 30 days as Amazon’s algorithm Re Indexes your content. For PPC, we usually see a stabilization and improvement in ACOS within the first 45 days as we harvest data and eliminate wasted spend.

Does Ecom Squire handle Brand Registry and hijacker removal?

Yes. We assist with the Brand Registry application process and provide active brand defense. Our team monitors your listings 24/7 to identify unauthorized resellers or "hijackers" and takes immediate action through Amazon’s reporting tools to protect your Buy Box.

What makes Ecom Squire different from using automated PPC software?

Software is a tool, not a strategy. While we use advanced data tools, every bid adjustment and keyword harvest is overseen by an expert "Squire." We provide the human intuition and market context that automated scripts miss, ensuring your budget is spent on High Converting intent rather than generic traffic.

Do I retain full ownership and access to my Seller Central account?

Absolutely. You maintain 100% ownership of your account and brand. You simply grant us "User Permissions" to manage specific Back End operations. We believe in total transparency, and you can monitor our work and your account's performance in real-time.

Can you help my brand expand into international Amazon marketplaces?

Expansion is one of our core specialties. We help brands scale into Amazon UK, EU, UAE and beyond. We handle the technical complexities of listing localization, global shipping logistics and international tax compliance so your expansion is seamless.
